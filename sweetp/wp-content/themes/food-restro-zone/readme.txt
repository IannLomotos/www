== Description ==

The Food Restro Zone Theme is a stylish, modern, and visually captivating lite theme designed specifically for food and beverage businesses like food restaurants, cafes, bistros, diners, takeaway joints, eateries, and also juice bars, tea houses, ice cream parlors, bakeries, and dessert cafés, making it ideal for a variety of culinary businesses. Whether you run a fine dining establishment, casual dining spot, fast food outlet, easter catering, themed party catering, destination event catering, cultural event dining, family reunion catering, social event catering, engagement party catering, bridal shower catering, baby shower catering, rooftop lounge, themed diner, or a gourmet kitchen, this theme offers the ideal digital platform to showcase your culinary brand online. Built with clean code and a mobile-responsive layout, it caters perfectly to family eateries, vegan cafés, burger houses, pizza places, food courts, and food trucks. With built-in customization tools and an intuitive structure, the theme allows restaurant owners to efficiently manage digital food menus, promote chef specials, announce happy hour deals, and enable table reservations, takeaway orders, or food delivery services. Its visual design includes elegant homepage sliders, high-resolution food image galleries, menu display sections, and interactive blocks for customer reviews, daily specials, lunch combos, and dinner menus. The theme is perfect for showcasing seasonal dishes, tasting menus, romantic dinner events, or promotional offers in an engaging way. It supports core features like online table booking, browse-by-category food items, reservation forms, order tracking, and contactless delivery information—integrating seamlessly with plugins like RestaurantPress for optimized menu management. Whether you’re operating a cozy corner café or a bustling downtown restaurant, the Food Restro Zone Theme helps you create a compelling, search-friendly online presence that attracts food lovers, increases reservations, and enhances your culinary brand’s visibility across major search engines.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Type in Food Restro Zone in the search form and press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.
3. Click on the 'Activate' button to use your new theme right away.
4. Navigate to Appearance > Customize in your admin panel and customize to taste.
